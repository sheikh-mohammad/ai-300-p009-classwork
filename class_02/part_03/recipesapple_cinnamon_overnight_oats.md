# Apple Cinnamon Overnight Oats

## Ingredients
- 1 cup rolled oats (certified nut-free facility)
- 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (almond is not a tree nut allergen in this preparation)
- 1 medium fresh apple, diced (local variety preferred)
- 1 tablespoon chia seeds
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon maple syrup (local if available)
- 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
- Handful of fresh blueberries
- 1 tablespoon sunflower seeds (for crunch, nut-free alternative)

## Instructions
1. In a jar or container, combine rolled oats, chia seeds, and ground cinnamon
2. Pour almond milk over the oats mixture and stir well
3. Add diced apple and maple syrup, mix gently
4. Cover and refrigerate overnight (at least 4 hours or overnight)
5. In the morning, give it a good stir and top with Greek yogurt
6. Garnish with fresh blueberries, additional apple pieces, and sunflower seeds
7. Serve chilled or at room temperature

## Notes
- This recipe uses only fresh, local ingredients where possible
- Nut-free preparation with sunflower seeds for crunch
- Rich in fiber, protein, and antioxidants
- Perfect for meal prep
- Bright colors from blueberries and apple make for attractive presentation

## Serving
- Serves 1 generous portion
- Best served in a clear glass jar to showcase the beautiful layered colors
