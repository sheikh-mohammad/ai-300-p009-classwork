# Berry & Honey Oat Breakfast Bowl

## Overview
A nourishing, allergy-safe breakfast featuring rolled oats topped with fresh seasonal berries, local honey, and creamy yogurt. Perfect for a high-quality start to your day.

## Ingredients

### Base
- 1/2 cup rolled oats (steel-cut or old-fashioned)
- 1 cup whole milk or plant-based alternative
- 1/4 cup water
- Pinch of sea salt

### Toppings
- 1/2 cup fresh mixed berries (blueberries, strawberries, raspberries)
- 2 tablespoons local honey
- 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 1 tablespoon ground flaxseed
- Fresh mint leaves (optional)

## Instructions

1. **Cook the oats:** In a small saucepan, combine oats, milk, water, and salt. Bring to a gentle boil over medium heat.

2. **Simmer:** Reduce heat to low and simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until oats reach desired consistency.

3. **Transfer:** Pour cooked oats into a serving bowl.

4. **Add toppings:** Layer with Greek yogurt, fresh berries, and drizzle with local honey.

5. **Finish:** Sprinkle ground flaxseed and fresh mint if desired.

6. **Serve:** Enjoy immediately while warm.

## Nutritional Highlights
- High in fiber and complex carbohydrates
- Rich in antioxidants from fresh berries
- Protein-rich with Greek yogurt
- Omega-3s from flaxseed

## Allergy Information
✅ **Nut-Free** | ✅ **Dairy-Free Option Available** | ✅ **Gluten-Free** (if using certified GF oats)

## Serving Suggestions
- Pairs well with herbal tea or fresh-squeezed juice
- Can be prepared ahead and reheated gently
- Customize berries based on seasonal availability
